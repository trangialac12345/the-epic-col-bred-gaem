📖 guide
🍞 bread

Ticks every 3s. Click for 15% of tick. Rapid clicks build a combo (up to ×5). Buildings add BpS.

🌟 golden bread

A 🌟 golden bread spawns randomly every 2–5 min. Click it within 30s to trigger Bread Frenzy — 1.5× bread for 90 seconds.

⚡ ore rush

Break a Ruby, Topaz, or Jewel tile in the mine to trigger Ore Rush — passive ore drops 10× faster for 30 seconds.

✨ lucky drops

Every passive ore tick has a 0.5% chance to drop a Lucky Crumb — instant +10× your current tick gain.

🏗️ building milestones

Every 10 of the same building unlocks a permanent +1% BpS bonus. Stack them for huge compounding gains.

⬛ the void

At 100,000 total ore, The Void triggers once. The game goes dark for 30s — then you get triple bread + 3 Jewel as reward.

🥖 powerloaf

1 Loaf/60s. 50 Loaves = +1 PL = +1% bread.

🔗 synergies

Require two or more specific upgrades to unlock. Each one combines systems for a unique compounding effect. Tagged SYNERGY in the shop.
